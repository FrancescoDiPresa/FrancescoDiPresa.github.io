I · Causal inference & econometrics

β = +0.30 *** effect on ratings 380k reviews · 9 firms · 940 clusters

The Role of Emotion in AI-Mediated Customer Service — MSc thesis

Built a text-based AI Intensity Index from ~380,000 Google Play reviews of online travel agencies (TF-IDF + logistic classifier, validated against Google Trends at r = 0.80), then estimated its causal effect on satisfaction with two-way fixed effects panel regressions, standard errors clustered at firm × month. AI adoption raises ratings, lifts sentiment, and reduces negative emotions — with placebo tests and parallel-trends checks supporting identification.
